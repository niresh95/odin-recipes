Oat chocolate bar

Description

Delicious no-bake oatmeal bars with a chocolaty peanut butter layer. I made about 100 dozen cookies for the holidays, and these were everyone's favorite!

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups quick cooking oats
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  1. Grease a pan.
  2. Melt butter on medium heat and Stir in brown sugar and vanilla.
  3. Cook over low heat until ingredients are well blended, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Press 1/2 of the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan. Reserve remaining oat mixture for topping.
  5. Meanwhile, melt chocolate chips and peanut butter in a small heavy sausepan over low heat, stirring frequently until smooth.
  6. Crumble remaining oat mixture over chocolate layer, pressing down gently. Cover and refrigerate fro 2 to 3 hours or overnight. Bring to room temperature before cutting into bars.
